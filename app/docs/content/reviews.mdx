## Review Process

Every project goes through review at two stages: after design submission and after build completion.

### What Reviewers Look For

**Design Review:**
- Is the project feasible with the proposed components?
- Is the BOM reasonable and correctly priced?
- Are there enough design journal entries showing thought and research?
- Does the project scope match the claimed tier?

**Build Review:**
- Did the builder complete what they proposed?
- Are work sessions documented with photos/videos?
- Do the logged hours reflect the actual work done?
- Are badge claims accurate?

### Review Outcomes

- **Approved** — Your project advances to the next stage (or is fully complete)
- **Changes Requested** — Reviewers found issues. Address their feedback and resubmit.
- **Rejected** — The project doesn't meet requirements. You can start a new project.

### Pending vs Confirmed Bits

When your **design** is approved, you receive **pending bits** — a preview of what you'll earn. These count toward the Stasis event invite threshold but cannot be spent on most shop items.

When your **build** is approved, pending bits convert to **confirmed bits** that are fully spendable.

> If your build is rejected, pending bits are cancelled.
