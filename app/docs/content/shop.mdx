## Shop

The Stasis shop lets you spend your earned bits on rewards and perks.

### How It Works

- Browse available items in the shop from your dashboard
- Each item has a bits cost
- Purchase items using your confirmed bits balance
- Some items (like the Stasis Event Invite) can be purchased with pending bits

### Bits Balance

Your balance is calculated from the currency ledger:

- **Earned bits** — Sum of all bits from approved projects and admin grants
- **Spent bits** — Sum of all shop purchases and deductions
- **Pending bits** — Bits from design-approved (but not yet build-approved) projects
- **Available balance** — Earned minus spent (pending bits only count for select items)

### Qualification Threshold

To qualify for the Stasis in-person event, you need to reach the bits threshold through your project work. Check the dashboard for the current threshold and your progress toward it.
