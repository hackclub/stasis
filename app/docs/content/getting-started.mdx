## Welcome to Stasis

Stasis is a hardware hackathon platform where you design and build real hardware projects, earn bits for your work, and get funded to bring your ideas to life.

### How It Works

1. **Create a project** — Describe what you want to build and add a Bill of Materials (BOM) for the parts you need
2. **Submit your design** — Once your design is ready, submit it for review by our team
3. **Get approved & start building** — After design approval, you'll receive pending bits and can begin your build
4. **Log your work** — Track your progress with journal entries and time logging
5. **Submit your build** — When you're done, submit for final review to earn your confirmed bits

### Key Concepts

- **Bits** — The Stasis currency. Earn bits by completing projects. Spend bits in the shop.
- **Tiers** — Projects are categorized into tiers based on complexity and time investment. Higher tiers earn more bits.
- **Badges** — Earn achievement badges for using specific hardware features like I2C, SPI, WiFi, and more.
- **BOM (Bill of Materials)** — The list of parts and costs for your project. Approved BOMs are funded by Stasis.
