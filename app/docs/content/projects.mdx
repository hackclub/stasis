## Projects

Projects are the core of Stasis. Each project goes through a two-stage review process: **Design** and **Build**.

### Creating a Project

From your dashboard, click "New Project" to get started. You'll need:

- A descriptive **title**
- A **description** of what you're building and why
- A **Bill of Materials** listing all parts, quantities, and costs
- Optionally, a **GitHub repository** link

### Design Stage

During the design stage, focus on planning your project:

- Research your components and ensure compatibility
- Create schematics or diagrams
- Write journal entries documenting your design decisions
- Add cart screenshots showing where you'll purchase parts

> Submit your design when you have a clear plan and complete BOM. Reviewers will check feasibility, cost, and scope.

### Build Stage

After design approval, you move into the build stage:

- Log **work sessions** with descriptions of what you accomplished
- Attach **photos and videos** of your progress
- Track your time accurately — this affects your tier placement
- Claim **badges** for hardware features you implement

### Tiers

| Tier | Hours | Bits |
|------|-------|------|
| Spark | 1–3h | 25 |
| Circuit | 3–10h | 75 |
| Board | 10–20h | 150 |
| System | 20–40h | 250 |
| Machine | 40h+ | 400 |

Your tier is determined by the total hours invested in your project.
