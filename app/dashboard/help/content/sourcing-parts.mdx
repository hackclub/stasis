

Welcome to part sourcing! This guide offers optimization tips for part selection and budgeting. Submit BOMs to #highway on Slack for community feedback.

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Research parts thoroughly
- Ask on Slack for advice on part selection

**Don't:**
- Purchase expensive oscilloscopes ($100+)
- Buy extra parts for future projects
- Buy overpriced modules on Amazon when cheaper alternatives exist elsewhere

All parts should be compiled into a BOM with specific links.

## Specific Parts

**General:** Reference parts from established projects like Adafruit to reduce unknowns.

**Modules (TP4056, LED matrices, etc):** AliExpress offers the cheapest pricing with typical 2–3 week shipping. Amazon is available for urgent needs.

**SBCs (Raspberry Pi, Orange Pi, etc):** Orange Pis are available via AliExpress. Consider repurposing old computers for non-space-sensitive applications.

**Microcontrollers (Arduino, RP2040, etc):** AliExpress is recommended. Note that Arduinos are outdated by current standards.

**PCBs:**
- **India:** SEEED Studio Fusion Service (accepts HCB cards, cheapest option)
- **Other countries:** JLCPCB (under $6 shipped for boards under 100x100mm)
- **US specifically:** PCBWAY (roughly half JLCPCB's cost due to tariff avoidance)
- ENIG is not permitted except for golden fingers, with case-by-case approval
- Edge plating is only allowed if functional
- Choose the cheapest option that yields working PCBs

## Country-Specific Notes

### Qatar
**Parts & Tools:**
- [Voltaat](https://www.voltaat.com/) (Electronics and 3D printers. Gives discounts to Qatar University students. Same day delivery to most of Qatar. Also has a makerspace. Located in Doha)
- [SmartQat](https://smartqat.com/) (Electronic components and tools and some chemistry things. Located in Al Rayan)
- [Al Annabi](https://alannabi.qa/product-category/) (Sells some electronics components. Located in Doha.)

**3D Printing:**
- [Voltaat](https://www.voltaat.com/) (Offers 3D printing services)
- [SmartQat](https://smartqat.com/) (Offers 3D printing training)

### Egypt

**Parts & Tools:**
- [RAM Electronics](https://www.ram-e-shop.com/) (Cytron certified, significantly lower pricing. Located in Downtown Cairo)
- [Flux Electronix](https://fluxelectronix.com/) (3D printer parts and electronics. Located in Downtown Cairo)
- [Lampatronics](https://lampatronics.com/) (Very competitive pricing, in many times it's cheaper than RAM. Located in Downtown Cairo)
- [Free Electronics](https://free-electronic.com/) (Diverse stock of components and tools, good prices. Located in Downtown Cairo)
- [Makers Electronics](https://makerselectronics.com/) (Very diverse stock of components and tools. Located in Alexandria, shipping to Cairo takes 3 days)
- [UGE One](https://uge-one.com/) (Very good to find specific rare chips and components. Located in Nasr City, Cairo)
- [IC Hat](https://ic-hat.com/) (Good to find rare chips and spare parts. Located in Downtown Cairo)

**PCBs:**
- [Metanoia](https://metanoia-eg.com/) (affordable local PCB fab located in Cairo)
- Free Electronics offers PCB design and fabrication.
- UGE One offers PCB design and fabrication.

**3D Printing:**
- Lampatronics offers 3D printing services.

## AliExpress Tips

- Welcome deals only apply once per account — don't budget assuming continued discounts
- Monitor estimated shipment dates (generally reliable by region)
- Compare total costs including shipping fees — low initial prices don't guarantee savings
- Recommended vendors: AITXEM ROBOT (microcontrollers), Trianglelab (3D printer parts)
