## Start/End Times
The event begins at 6:30PM on Friday, May 15th, but attendees can arrive as early as 3PM.

The event ends at around 12-1PM on Monday, May 18th.

If you must arrive late due to an unavoidable conflict such as an AP exam, that's all good, just make sure to let us know in advance.

## ✈️ Flights
Most people will fly into Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS). We will have staff there to meet you and Uber you to the venue.

We recommend booking flights that land at least 1 hour before the start of the event and depart at least 3 hours after the event ends.

If you bought travel stipends in the shop, we will reimburse you that amount. If you need us to book your travel for you, please contact an organizer (only possible if you buy more travel stipend than the cost of your flight).

## Pre/Post Event Accommodation

If you need to arrive a day early or leave a day late, pre and post event accommodation is available for purchase in the shop.

## Trains 🚆, Buses 🚌, and Cars 🚗
If you are traveling to Stasis by means of land transport, you can get reimbursed by using the [Gas Fund](https://gas.hackclub.com). Keep in mind the Gas Fund is unlikely to cover 100% of expenses, so you can buy additional travel stipends in the Stasis shop.

The venue address is Arena Hall, 108 W Gibson St, Austin, TX 78704.

## Visas
If you need a visa, please apply for a visa invitation letter [here](https://visas.hackclub.com/events/stasis/apply)
