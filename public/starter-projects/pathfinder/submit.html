Submit Your Pathfinder on Stasis

Here's how to get your Pathfinder project reviewed and funded through Stasis.

1. Create an account on Stasis

Head to stasis and sign in with your Hack Club account. If you don't have one yet, you'll be able to create one during sign-in.

2. Create a new project

From your dashboard, click "New Project". Give it a name, select Pathfinder as the starter project, and add a short description of what you're building.

3. Submit your design for review

Upload your KiCad schematic and PCB files to a GitHub repo and link it in your project. Add a Bill of Materials (BOM) with the parts you need. Then hit "Submit for Design Review". A reviewer will check your schematic and PCB layout.

4. Buy your PCB!

Once your project is approved, you'll get an email with an HCB grant card so you can buy your PCB. Click here to learn how to buy your PCB once you get your card!

5. Don't forget to journal your building as well

Remember you still need to journal building your project! Include photos/videos of your finished project in your GitHub repo and submit your project again. Only when you do this will you get your bits :)
